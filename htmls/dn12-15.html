Cyber Cat

Monday, November 27, 2023

Monday Mini-Update: Retroclear Gear!

Hey there, heroes!

It's Cyber Monday! This year, we have a selection of mysterious Retroclear gear available for purchase!

Available this week from the Book of Lore, Retroclear gear comes in both normal and Color Custom (Dragon Eye) options!

Check them out before they leave!

Also today, there's been a few updates and adjustments!

  • Items in the Bank interface now show their level.
  • The candle in the Bank building has been moved down a bit to prevent a flickering flame.
  • Item descriptions now show stackable item quantities.
  • Moved the filigree in the Inventory UI so that it no longer covers up text.
  • Fixed an issue where DoT damage pop ups could overlap healing pop ups.
  • Fixed an issue where attacks could sometimes overlap healing pop ups.
  • Fixed an issue where Bonus wasn't calculated properly in some instances.

And that's all for today! A chilly new challenge is coming later this week to the Arena at the Edge of Time!
